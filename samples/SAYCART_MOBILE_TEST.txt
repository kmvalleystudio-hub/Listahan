SayCart — copy this to your phone (Notes, email, or cloud)
============================================================

BEFORE YOU TEST (on your computer)
------------------------------------
1. Open the SayCart project folder in a terminal.
2. Run:  npx expo start
3. Voice (mic) needs a DEVELOPMENT BUILD, not plain Expo Go.
   - Android: plug in phone with USB debugging, then:
     npx expo run:android
   - Or install a dev build you already made, then connect to Metro.

4. Phone and PC must reach Metro:
   - Same Wi-Fi: use the URL shown in the terminal (exp://...).
   - Or run:  npx expo start --tunnel   (slower but works across networks).
   - USB only: on PC run  adb reverse tcp:8081 tcp:8081  then open the app.

OPEN THE APP ON THE PHONE
-------------------------
- Launch SayCart (dev build).
- If you see a red "can't load script" error, Metro isn't reachable — fix Wi-Fi/tunnel/adb reverse, then reload.

TRY BULK LIST BY VOICE
----------------------
1. Open any list (or create one).
2. Tap the orange "Bulk List by Voice" banner.
3. Tap the big mic → speak ONE of the lines below → tap mic again to stop.

LINE A (use the word AND between items):
one milk and two coffee and three lemons and one mayonnaise and three eggs

LINE B (commas also work):
3 bear brand, 2 pancit canton, 1 coffee, 5 apples

Expected: each piece becomes its own row; quantity is a NUMBER in Qty, not "One" in the name.

TRY SINGLE-ITEM VOICE
---------------------
Add Item → mic next to the name field (dev build only).

TIP
---
If recognition is wrong, speak a bit slower and use the format:
  quantity FIRST, then product name, then AND, then next item.
